Steel and Concrete

Since steel structures are light structures heavy foundation is not required compared to concrete. The construction time of steel is less. Formworks are not required. Steel structures are easily assembled and dissembled also changes can be made at the last minute. Steels are eco-friendly, reusing and recycling is possible. Steel has a high scarp value.
